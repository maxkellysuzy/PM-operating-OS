## Key Takeaways

- **New Suzy is at or near parity** with Classic on core question types, audience demographics, and results/analysis basics.
- **New Suzy's differentiators** are AI-native features: AI Chat Study Generation, Chat with data, AI insights generation, Stories Studio, Research Planner/Brief, Projects, Version History, and self-serve Suzy Speaks with AI Discussion Guide generation.
- **Classic-only (today) gaps to watch in New Suzy:** Video open-end, Charted PowerPoint export, Reviewable Status and Respondent Removal, Horizontal Demographic Data File, Data Explorer, Open-end response quality flag, Suzy Live, Suzy Connect, Global Studies, and Question & Survey Templates — all marked "Coming soon."
- **Vertical Data File** is the one capability currently flagged "Not available" on New Suzy with no stated roadmap commitment on this sheet.
- **Suzy Speaks** self-serve is cheaper (20 credits vs. 20 + 20 MS credits), adds AI Discussion Guide Generation and AI Chat with results, and has a heavy "By end of April" delivery push for quotas, Auto-Assign, feasibility checks, and exports.
