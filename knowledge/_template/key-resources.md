## Decision-making checklist

Before committing to a feature or initiative:

1. Does it align with a strategic pillar?
2. Which customer segment benefits most?
3. What metric will it move, and by how much?
4. What are we deprioritizing to make room?
5. What's the smallest version we can ship to learn?
