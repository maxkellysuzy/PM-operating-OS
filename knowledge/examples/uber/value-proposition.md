# Value Proposition — Uber

> Sources: Uber 2024 10-K, public communications

## Target Value Prop

One app to get a ride, order food, or ship anything — powered by the world's largest on-demand mobility and delivery network, available in 10,500+ cities across 70+ countries.

## Product Design Principles

1. **Reliability above all** — A ride or delivery that arrives predictably and on-time is the core product. ETAs, driver matching, and surge pricing all optimize for reliability at scale.
2. **Marketplace balance** — Uber's product is the marketplace itself. Every feature must consider both sides: consumer experience and earner economics. Imbalance (too few drivers, too-low pay) degrades the product for everyone.
3. **Platform leverage** — Build once, deploy across. Mapping, matching, pricing, payments, and safety systems serve Mobility, Delivery, and Freight simultaneously. Shared infrastructure = operating leverage.
4. **Safety as a feature** — Real-time ride tracking, driver background checks, in-app emergency button, RideCheck (crash detection), and identity verification are product differentiators, not just compliance requirements.

## Key Differentiators

- Global scale: 10,500+ cities, 70+ countries, 171M MAPCs — the largest on-demand mobility/delivery network
- Multi-product platform in one app: rides, food delivery, grocery, package delivery, transit, car rental
- Uber One membership creates cross-platform lock-in — members use both Mobility and Delivery, spend 4x more, churn 50% less
- Marketplace data at scale: billions of trips/year power best-in-class ETA prediction, dynamic pricing, and supply-demand matching
- AV-ready: positioned as the demand aggregator for autonomous vehicle fleets (Waymo, Cruise partnerships)
