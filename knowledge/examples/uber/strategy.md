# Strategy — Uber

> Sources: Uber 2024 10-K, Q4 2024 Earnings Call, Investor Day presentations

## Mission

Reimagine the way the world moves for the better. Uber connects consumers with drivers, couriers, merchants, and public transit — powering movement and delivery at global scale.

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Mobility leadership** — Remain the global leader in ride-hailing. Expand from ride-hailing into new verticals: Uber for Teens, Uber for Business, Uber Reserve, and autonomous vehicles. 171M monthly active platform consumers (MAPCs) as of Q4 2024.
2. **Delivery growth and profitability** — Scale Uber Eats and grocery/convenience delivery. Focus on merchant selection, delivery speed, and basket size. Delivery gross bookings grew 18% YoY in Q4 2024.
3. **Platform synergies and cross-sell** — One app, multiple services. Users who use both Mobility and Delivery spend 4x more and churn 50% less. Uber One membership (~30M members) drives cross-platform engagement.
4. **Advertising** — Build a high-margin advertising business on top of the marketplace. Ads revenue reached a $1B+ annualized run rate in 2024. Sponsored listings, post-checkout ads, and in-app placements for merchants and brands.

## Phased Approach

- **Phase 1 (Done):** Win ride-hailing globally and establish Uber Eats as the #1 or #2 delivery platform in most markets. Achieved GAAP profitability in FY2023.
- **Phase 2 (Now):** Drive platform compounding — cross-sell across Mobility + Delivery + Freight, grow Uber One, build advertising, and expand into autonomous vehicles.
- **Phase 3 (Next):** Become the "operating system for everyday life" — integrate AV fleets, autonomous delivery, public transit, and last-mile logistics into a single platform.

## Quarterly Priorities (as of Q4 2024)

- Scale Uber One membership (30M+ members, growing 60%+ YoY)
- Grow advertising revenue past $1B annual run rate — target is a multi-billion-dollar business
- Autonomous vehicle partnerships (Waymo in Phoenix and SF, Cruise, others) — bring AV supply onto the Uber platform
- Improve Delivery profitability through merchant ads, order batching, and higher basket sizes
