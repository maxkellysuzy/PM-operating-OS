# Metrics and Targets — Uber

> Sources: Uber Q4 2024 Earnings, 2024 10-K

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | FY2024 Actual | Target / Trend |
|--------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Gross Bookings | Total dollar value of Mobility rides + Delivery orders + Freight | $162.8B FY2024 | ~20% YoY growth |
| Revenue | Net revenue after driver/courier payouts | $43.9B FY2024 | Growing ~18% YoY |
| MAPCs | Monthly Active Platform Consumers | 171M (Q4 2024) | Growing — audience expansion via Uber One, Teens, Business |
| Trips | Total trips across Mobility + Delivery | 11.1B FY2024 (~30.4M/day) | Growing with engagement per MAPC |
| Adjusted EBITDA | Core profitability metric (excl. SBC, depreciation) | $6.5B FY2024 | Growing ~60%+ YoY; margin expanding |
| GAAP Net Income | Bottom-line profitability | $9.9B FY2024 (includes gains) | First full-year GAAP profit in FY2023; sustained |
| Free Cash Flow | Operating cash flow minus capex | $6.9B FY2024 | Rapidly growing — supports buybacks |
| Uber One Members | Paid membership subscribers | 30M+ (Q4 2024) | Growing 60%+ YoY |
| Ads Revenue | Advertising business annualized run rate | $1B+ (annualized Q4 2024) | Target: multi-billion-dollar business |

## Financial Targets

- Gross bookings grew 20% YoY (constant currency) in FY2024 to $162.8B
- Adjusted EBITDA of $6.5B in FY2024, up from $4.1B in FY2023 — 60%+ growth
- Free cash flow of $6.9B in FY2024 — funding $7B+ share buyback program
- Mobility segment: 25%+ Adjusted EBITDA margins on gross bookings
- Delivery segment: reached Adjusted EBITDA profitability in 2023, margins expanding
- Long-term: mid-to-high teens Adjusted EBITDA growth annually, sustained FCF expansion
