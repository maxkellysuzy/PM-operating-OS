# Customer Segments — Uber

> Sources: Uber 2024 10-K, Q4 2024 Earnings, public disclosures

## Primary Segments

### Segment 1: Mobility Riders
- **Who:** 171M monthly active platform consumers globally (includes overlap with Delivery). Broad demographic: commuters, travelers, nightlife, airport runs.
- **Needs:** Reliable pickup within minutes, predictable pricing, safety features, cashless payment, multiple vehicle options (UberX, Comfort, XL, Black).
- **Pain points:** Surge pricing during peak demand, wait times in suburban areas, driver quality inconsistency, safety concerns (addressed via in-app features).

### Segment 2: Delivery Consumers (Uber Eats)
- **Who:** Consumers ordering food, grocery, and convenience delivery through Uber Eats. ~$18.7B in Delivery gross bookings in Q4 2024 alone.
- **Needs:** Wide restaurant/merchant selection, fast delivery (under 30 min), accurate ETAs, affordable delivery fees, easy reordering.
- **Pain points:** High fees (delivery + service + tip), order accuracy issues, cold food from long deliveries, restaurant availability gaps in suburban/rural areas.

### Segment 3: Drivers and Couriers (Earners)
- **Who:** ~7.8M drivers and couriers globally earned on Uber in Q4 2024. Mix of full-time earners and part-time gig workers.
- **Needs:** Flexible earning opportunities, predictable income, fair pay, low vehicle costs, fast access to earnings (Instant Pay), safety.
- **Pain points:** Fuel/maintenance costs, earnings unpredictability, regulatory uncertainty (gig worker classification — AB5 in California, EU directive), wait times between trips.

### Segment 4: Merchants (Uber Eats)
- **Who:** Restaurants, grocery stores, convenience stores, and retail merchants on Uber Eats. 1M+ merchants globally.
- **Needs:** Incremental revenue, new customer acquisition, operational simplicity (order management), marketing/visibility tools (Sponsored Listings).
- **Pain points:** High commission rates (15–30%), dependency on platform for customer relationship, cannibalization of in-store sales.

### Segment 5: Enterprise / Uber for Business
- **Who:** Companies using Uber for employee travel, meal programs, event logistics. 200K+ enterprise clients.
- **Needs:** Centralized billing, travel policy enforcement, expense management integration, reliability for events.
- **Pain points:** Cost control, duty of care compliance, integration with corporate travel platforms.

## Personas

- **Daily Commuter:** Uses Uber 3-5x/week for work commute. High LTV, price-sensitive. Uber One member.
- **Weekend Foodie:** Orders Uber Eats 2-3x/week. Browses for new restaurants. High basket size, responsive to promotions.
- **Gig Driver:** Drives 30+ hours/week on Uber and Lyft simultaneously. Optimizes for highest earnings per hour.
