# Value Proposition — Netflix

> Sources: Netflix 2024 10-K, public communications

## Target Value Prop

The world's best entertainment — original stories, films, live events, and games — all in one place, personalized for every member, at a price point for every budget.

## Product Design Principles

1. **Content is king** — The #1 reason people subscribe is great content. Everything else (UI, recommendations, pricing) amplifies great content but can't substitute for it.
2. **Personalization everywhere** — From the home screen to artwork to recommendations, every surface adapts to individual taste profiles. Netflix manages 700M+ taste profiles.
3. **Effortless discovery** — Reduce the time between "I want to watch something" and pressing play. Autoplay previews, "Top 10" lists, and percentage-match scoring all serve this goal.
4. **Global-local** — Build a platform that works globally but feels local. Korean content plays globally (Squid Game: 265M views); local-language content drives growth in each market.

## Key Differentiators

- Largest original content library globally — more Emmy nominations than any network/streamer in 2024
- 301.7M paid subscribers across 190+ countries — unmatched global scale
- Recommendation engine powered by 700M+ profiles and billions of viewing hours of signal
- Cultural impact — Netflix originals consistently dominate social conversation globally
- Price-tiered access: ad-supported ($6.99), Standard ($15.49), Premium ($22.99) — broadest accessibility range among premium streamers
