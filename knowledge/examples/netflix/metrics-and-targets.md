# Metrics and Targets — Netflix

> Sources: Netflix Q4 2024 Shareholder Letter, 2024 10-K

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | FY2024 Actual | Target / Trend |
|--------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| Paid Memberships | Total global paid subscriber households | 301.7M (Q4 2024) | Stopped reporting quarterly in 2025; focus shifts to revenue |
| Revenue | Total annual revenue | $39.0B FY2024 | Targeting $43–44B in FY2025 |
| Operating Margin | Operating income / Revenue | 26.7% FY2024 | 28%+ target for FY2025 |
| ARM (UCAN) | Avg Revenue per Membership, US & Canada / month | $17.44 (Q4 2024) | Growing via price increases and plan mix |
| ARM (Global) | Avg Revenue per Membership, global / month | $10.81 (Q4 2024) | Growing but diluted by lower-ARPU international markets |
| Ad Tier MAUs | Monthly active users on Standard with Ads | 70M+ (Q4 2024) | Rapid growth; targeting scale for ad revenue |
| Content Spend | Cash spent on content (amortization basis) | ~$17B FY2024 | Relatively stable; growing slower than revenue for margin expansion |
| Free Cash Flow | Operating cash flow minus capex | $6.9B FY2024 | Growing; used for buybacks and content investment |

## Financial Targets

- Revenue grew 16% YoY in FY2024 to $39.0B
- Operating income of $10.4B in FY2024 (26.7% margin), up from 21.0% in FY2023
- Free cash flow of $6.9B, up from $6.9B in FY2023
- Share buybacks of $6.2B in FY2024 — returning capital to shareholders
- FY2025 guidance: $43.5–44.5B revenue, 28%+ operating margin
- Long-term: sustain double-digit revenue growth, expand operating margins to 30%+
