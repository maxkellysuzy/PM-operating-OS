# Strategy — Netflix

> Sources: Netflix 2024 10-K, Q4 2024 Shareholder Letter, Investor Communications

## Mission

Entertain the world. Netflix aims to be the best entertainment service globally, helping creators around the world find a global audience.

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Content excellence** — Invest heavily in original content across film, series, unscripted, animation, and games. Content spending was ~$17B in FY2024. Win more "viewing share" in every market.
2. **Advertising tier growth** — Scale the ad-supported plan launched in Nov 2022. Ad tier reached 70M+ MAUs globally by Q4 2024, up from 40M in Q2 2024. Build a world-class ad tech stack (in-house ad server launched 2024).
3. **Engagement beyond video** — Expand into live events (NFL Christmas games, Jake Paul vs. Tyson), games, and interactive experiences to increase time spent and cultural relevance.
4. **Revenue growth and margin expansion** — Drive revenue growth via pricing, ads, and paid sharing. Expand operating margins toward 28%+ (achieved 26.7% in FY2024).

## Phased Approach

- **Phase 1 (Done):** Build the world's largest streaming platform — 301.7M paid memberships as of Q4 2024 across 190+ countries.
- **Phase 2 (Now):** Diversify revenue (ads + paid sharing), expand into live and games, drive engagement per member.
- **Phase 3 (Next):** Become the default entertainment platform globally — owning the cultural conversation across video, live events, and games.

## Quarterly Priorities (as of Q4 2024)

- Scale ad tier to become a meaningful revenue contributor (targeting parity with peers on ad tech capabilities)
- Grow engagement through live programming (NFL, WWE Raw starting Jan 2025)
- Stop reporting quarterly subscriber numbers starting Q1 2025 — shift investor focus to revenue and operating income
- Drive ARM (Average Revenue per Member) growth via plan mix and regional pricing
