# Key Learnings — Shopify

> Sources: Shopify earnings calls 2023–2024, shareholder letters, public communications

## Recent Experiment Results

- **Logistics divestiture (2023):** Shopify sold its logistics/fulfillment business (Deliverr/SFN) to Flexport after ~$2B in acquisitions and investments. Key learning: owning physical logistics is capital-intensive and distracted from Shopify's core software platform strength. Free cash flow margin improved dramatically post-divestiture (from negative to ~19%).
- **Checkout Extensibility (2023–2024):** Replacing the legacy checkout.liquid with a composable, app-based checkout for Plus merchants. Early results: merchants using checkout extensions see higher conversion rates. This unlocked enterprise migrations from Salesforce Commerce Cloud and Magento.
- **Shopify Editions (biannual releases):** Bundling 100+ features into two major launches per year (Winter and Summer Editions) creates marketing moments and drives merchant engagement. More effective at driving awareness than continuous deployment alone.

## Focus Shifts

- **From logistics ambitions to software focus:** The Flexport deal was the most significant strategic pivot — Shopify re-centered on being a commerce software platform, not an Amazon-like logistics company.
- **From SMB-only to enterprise:** Shopify Plus is the fastest-growing segment. Enterprise brands migrating from legacy platforms to Shopify validates the upmarket strategy.
- **From storefront to commerce infrastructure:** Shop Pay, Shopify Payments, and Shopify Capital are now the primary growth drivers. Merchant Solutions (75% of revenue) is larger than subscriptions.

## What We Know Now

- Integrated payments (Shopify Payments) are the single biggest driver of Merchant Solutions revenue and merchant stickiness — 61% GPV penetration and growing
- Shop Pay's off-platform expansion (available on Meta, Google) turns Shopify from a merchant tool into buyer-side infrastructure — this is strategically critical
- The "build vs. buy vs. partner" decision on logistics was the right call — Flexport partnership delivers similar merchant value without capital intensity
- AI tools (Shopify Magic — product descriptions, image generation, Sidekick chat) are table-stakes for merchant retention but not yet a meaningful upsell driver
- Plus merchants have significantly higher LTV and lower churn — the enterprise bet is paying off
