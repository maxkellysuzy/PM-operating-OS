# Metrics and Targets — Shopify

> Sources: Shopify Q4 2024 Earnings, 2024 10-K

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | FY2024 Actual | Target / Trend |
|--------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| GMV | Gross Merchandise Volume — total value of orders processed | ~$270B+ FY2024 | Growing ~20%+ YoY |
| Revenue | Total annual revenue | $8.9B FY2024 | 25% YoY growth in FY2024 |
| Merchant Solutions Revenue | Payments, capital, shipping, etc. | ~$6.7B (~75% of total) | Growing faster than subscriptions |
| Subscription Solutions Revenue | Monthly subscription fees | ~$2.2B (~25% of total) | Steady growth from plan pricing and Plus |
| Gross Payments Volume (GPV) | GMV processed through Shopify Payments | 61% of GMV (Q4 2024) | Expanding — drives Merchant Solutions growth |
| Free Cash Flow Margin | FCF / Revenue | ~19% FY2024 | Targeting sustained double-digit FCF margins |
| MRR | Monthly Recurring Revenue | ~$175M+ (Q4 2024) | Growing via new merchants + Plus upsells |
| Shopify Plus Merchants | Enterprise-tier merchant count | ~18,800+ | Growing as enterprise migration accelerates |

## Financial Targets

- Revenue grew 25% YoY in FY2024 to $8.9B
- Free cash flow of ~$1.7B in FY2024 (~19% FCF margin), a dramatic improvement from cash-burn years (2020-2022)
- Gross margin ~51% — stable, with Merchant Solutions at lower margins offset by high-margin Subscription Solutions
- Divested logistics business (Deliverr/SFN) in 2023 to focus on core commerce platform — improved margins significantly
- Targeting sustained 20%+ revenue growth with expanding free cash flow margins
