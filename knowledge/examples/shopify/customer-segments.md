# Customer Segments — Shopify

> Sources: Shopify 2024 10-K, public earnings disclosures, investor communications

## Primary Segments

### Segment 1: Small and New Merchants (Basic Plan)
- **Who:** First-time entrepreneurs, side hustlers, dropshippers, small DTC brands. Basic plan at $39/mo. Millions of merchants globally.
- **Needs:** Easy-to-use store builder, low upfront cost, reliable payments, basic shipping tools, mobile management.
- **Pain points:** High competition in their niche, limited marketing budget, fulfillment complexity, difficulty driving traffic beyond their store.

### Segment 2: Growing Merchants (Shopify / Advanced Plans)
- **Who:** Established businesses doing $100K–$10M+ in annual revenue. Need more sophisticated tools: analytics, automation, international selling, staff accounts.
- **Needs:** Multi-channel selling (social, marketplace, POS), advanced reporting, international expansion tools (Shopify Markets), lower transaction fees.
- **Pain points:** Operational complexity as they scale, inventory management across channels, cross-border tax/duties, cash flow timing (addressed by Shopify Capital).

### Segment 3: Enterprise / Shopify Plus
- **Who:** Large brands and high-volume merchants ($10M+ annual GMV). ~18,800+ Plus merchants. Brands like Gymshark, Heinz, Supreme, Mattel.
- **Needs:** Customizable checkout (Checkout Extensibility), headless/composable commerce (Hydrogen), B2B/wholesale, dedicated support, SLAs, multi-store management.
- **Pain points:** Migration complexity from legacy platforms (Magento, Salesforce Commerce Cloud), need for custom integrations, enterprise procurement processes.

### Segment 4: Buyers / Consumers (Shop App / Shop Pay)
- **Who:** 150M+ Shop App users and Shop Pay users. Consumers who buy from Shopify-powered stores.
- **Needs:** Fast, trusted checkout (Shop Pay has 1.7x higher conversion vs. guest checkout), order tracking, product discovery.
- **Pain points:** Didn't historically know they were "shopping on Shopify" — Shop App builds that awareness and relationship.

## Personas

- **First-Time Founder:** Launching a DTC brand from their apartment. Chose Shopify because it's the default recommendation. Needs hand-holding on setup, marketing, and first sale.
- **Scaling DTC Brand:** $1M+ revenue, 3-5 employees, selling across website + Instagram + Amazon. Needs automation, better analytics, and international expansion.
- **Enterprise Brand Manager:** Migrating from Magento to Shopify Plus. Needs composable commerce, API flexibility, and guaranteed uptime.
