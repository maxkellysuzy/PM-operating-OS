# Strategy — Shopify

> Sources: Shopify 2024 10-K, Q4 2024 Earnings Call, public investor communications

## Mission

Make commerce better for everyone. Shopify builds the tools, infrastructure, and services that let entrepreneurs start, grow, and manage a business of any size.

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Be the commerce operating system** — Power the entire merchant journey: storefront, checkout, payments (Shop Pay), shipping, capital (Shopify Capital), and B2B/wholesale. Expand from "online store builder" to end-to-end commerce platform.
2. **Win enterprise (Shopify Plus)** — Move upmarket to capture larger merchants and brands. Shopify Plus powers brands like Gymshark, Allbirds, Supreme. Checkout extensibility and composable commerce are key.
3. **Expand buyer-side experiences** — Shop App (150M+ users) and Shop Pay (accelerated checkout available on and off Shopify) turn Shopify into a consumer-facing brand, not just a merchant tool.
4. **Global expansion** — Shopify Markets and Managed Markets simplify cross-border selling. International is a massive growth lever — ~35% of Shopify merchants' revenue comes from cross-border.

## Phased Approach

- **Phase 1 (Done):** Become the default platform for SMB e-commerce — millions of merchants in 175+ countries, ~$270B+ GMV in FY2024.
- **Phase 2 (Now):** Expand to enterprise, offline (POS), B2B, and international. Grow Merchant Solutions (payments, capital, shipping) as the primary revenue driver.
- **Phase 3 (Next):** Commerce infrastructure layer — Shop Pay becomes the checkout of the internet, Shopify becomes the commerce OS for any selling surface (social, marketplaces, retail, B2B).

## Quarterly Priorities (as of Q4 2024)

- Drive Merchant Solutions revenue growth (75%+ of total revenue, growing faster than Subscription Solutions)
- Scale Shopify Payments penetration (61% of GMV processed via Shopify Payments in Q4 2024)
- Grow Shop Pay's off-platform presence (available on Meta, Google, and as accelerated checkout across the web)
- Expand Shopify Plus merchant count and ARPU
