# Example Knowledge Folders

Real-world examples showing how to fill in your PM-OS knowledge layer, using **publicly available data** from big tech investor reports.

## Sources

All data in these examples comes from public filings and disclosures:
- SEC 10-K annual reports and 10-Q quarterly filings
- Quarterly earnings call transcripts
- Investor Day presentations
- Company press releases and shareholder letters

These are illustrative — they show how a PM at each company *might* structure their knowledge folder. They are not exhaustive or official.

## Companies

| Folder | Company | Business Model | Why It's Useful |
|--------|---------|---------------|-----------------|
| `spotify/` | Spotify | Subscription + ads, two-sided marketplace | Freemium conversion, creator/listener dynamics |
| `netflix/` | Netflix | Streaming + content, ad-tier expansion | Content investment ROI, subscriber growth strategy |
| `shopify/` | Shopify | Commerce platform for SMBs | Platform economics, merchant success metrics |
| `uber/` | Uber | Multi-sided marketplace, multi-product | Marketplace dynamics, cross-sell, unit economics |

## How to Use

1. Browse these examples to see how the template files get filled in
2. Copy `_template/` (not these examples) for your own product
3. Use these as inspiration for the level of detail and strategic framing
