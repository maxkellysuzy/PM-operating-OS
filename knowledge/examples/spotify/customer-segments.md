# Customer Segments — Spotify

> Sources: Spotify 2024 10-K, Investor Day 2024, public earnings disclosures

## Primary Segments

### Segment 1: Free (Ad-Supported) Users
- **Who:** ~412M monthly active users (MAUs) on the free tier globally (Q4 2024). Skew younger, mobile-first, price-sensitive.
- **Needs:** Access to a massive music catalog, discovery of new music, easy-to-use mobile app.
- **Pain points:** Ad interruptions, no offline listening, limited skips. Churn risk from YouTube Music and free alternatives.

### Segment 2: Premium Subscribers
- **Who:** ~263M paying subscribers (Q4 2024). Broad demographic, higher engagement, lower churn. Individual, Duo, Family, and Student plans.
- **Needs:** Ad-free listening, offline downloads, high-quality audio, cross-device experience, audiobooks access.
- **Pain points:** Price sensitivity to increases (prices raised in 2023 and 2024), desire for HiFi audio quality, podcast discovery friction.

### Segment 3: Creators (Artists, Podcasters, Authors)
- **Who:** 10M+ artists and creators on the platform. Range from independent bedroom artists to major labels.
- **Needs:** Reach new fans, earn revenue (streams, merch, tickets), access analytics (Spotify for Artists), promotional tools (Marquee, Discovery Mode).
- **Pain points:** Per-stream payouts perceived as low (~$0.003–0.005/stream), discoverability in a catalog of 100M+ tracks, competition for playlist placement.

### Segment 4: Advertisers
- **Who:** Brands and agencies buying audio, video, and display ads across Spotify's free tier and podcasts.
- **Needs:** Reach Gen Z and millennial audiences at scale, targeting via Spotify's first-party listening data, measurable outcomes.
- **Pain points:** Audio ad creative is harder to produce than display, measurement/attribution still maturing vs. social platforms.

## Personas

- **Casual Listener (Free):** Plays music in the background during commute or work. Low intent to pay. Conversion target.
- **Power User (Premium):** 2+ hours/day, curates playlists, listens to podcasts, uses offline mode. High retention, high LTV.
- **Emerging Artist:** <10K monthly listeners, relies on algorithmic playlists (Discover Weekly, Release Radar) for growth.
