# Metrics and Targets — Spotify

> Sources: Spotify Q4 2024 Earnings, 2024 10-K, Investor Day 2024

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Definition | FY2024 Actual | Target / Trend |
|--------|-----------|---------------|----------------|
| MAUs | Monthly Active Users (free + premium) | 675M | Continued growth, 1B long-term aspiration |
| Premium Subscribers | Paid subscribers across all plans | 263M | Grow via price increases + new markets |
| Premium ARPU | Average revenue per Premium user/month | €4.45 (Q4 2024) | Increasing via price raises and plan mix |
| Gross Margin | (Revenue - COGS) / Revenue | 32.2% (Q4 2024) | Target 40%+ medium-term |
| Ad Revenue | Revenue from ad-supported tier + podcast ads | ~€1.8B FY2024 (~12% of total) | Scale to 20%+ of revenue via SAX |
| Free-to-Premium Conversion | % of free MAUs converting to paid | ~39% (263M/675M) | Improve with audiobook bundling |
| Operating Income | GAAP operating profit | €1.4B FY2024 (first full-year profit) | Sustain and grow |
| Revenue | Total annual revenue | €15.7B FY2024 | ~15-20% YoY growth |

## Financial Targets

- First full-year GAAP operating profit in FY2024 (~€1.4B operating income)
- Gross margin expansion from 26.7% (Q4 2023) to 32.2% (Q4 2024); targeting 40%+ medium-term
- Revenue grew 19% YoY in FY2024 to €15.7B
- Free cash flow of €1.6B in FY2024, up from €0.7B in FY2023
- Aiming for €100B+ revenue long-term (per CEO Daniel Ek's public ambition)
