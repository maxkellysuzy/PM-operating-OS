# Value Proposition — Spotify

> Sources: Spotify 2024 10-K, Investor Day, public communications

## Target Value Prop

All the audio you love — music, podcasts, and audiobooks — personalized for you, available anywhere, with the best free tier in streaming or an ad-free Premium experience.

## Product Design Principles

1. **Personalization at scale** — Every listener gets a unique experience powered by ML (Discover Weekly, AI DJ, daylist). Personalization drives engagement and retention.
2. **Frictionless cross-platform** — Same library, same queue, same experience across mobile, desktop, car, smart speakers, and wearables via Spotify Connect.
3. **Creator-first tools** — Give artists and podcasters the analytics and promotional tools to grow their audience directly, making Spotify the best platform for creators.
4. **Audio-first, not audio-only** — Start with audio as the core medium but support video podcasts, canvas loops, and visual discovery when it enhances the experience.

## Key Differentiators

- Largest music streaming platform globally by MAU (675M) and subscribers (263M)
- Best personalization engine in audio streaming (Discover Weekly, Release Radar, AI DJ feature)
- Only platform combining music + podcasts + audiobooks at scale in a single app
- Free tier as a growth engine — most competitors lack a competitive free offering
- Spotify for Artists and marketplace tools (Marquee, Discovery Mode) create a unique two-sided platform dynamic
