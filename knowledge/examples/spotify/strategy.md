# Strategy — Spotify

> Sources: Spotify 2024 10-K, Q4 2024 Earnings Call, 2024 Investor Day

## Mission

Unlock the potential of human creativity by giving a million creative artists the opportunity to live off their art and billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it.

## Strategic Pillars

1. **Audio-first platform** — Expand beyond music into podcasts, audiobooks, and emerging audio formats to become the default audio companion. Audiobooks launched in 2023 across 9 markets; bundled into Premium in 2024.
2. **Two-sided marketplace** — Build tools for creators (Spotify for Artists, Marquee, Discovery Mode) that let artists reach fans directly while generating ad and marketplace revenue.
3. **Monetization diversification** — Grow advertising revenue and marketplace revenue alongside subscriptions. Ad-supported revenue was ~12% of total in FY2024, with ambitions to scale significantly via Spotify Ad Exchange (SAX).
4. **Margin expansion** — Drive operating leverage through personalization at scale (ML-driven recommendations), podcast cost rationalization (shift from exclusive to licensed), and pricing power.

## Phased Approach

- **Phase 1 (Done):** Win music streaming globally — 675M+ MAUs, 263M+ Premium subscribers as of Q4 2024.
- **Phase 2 (Now):** Expand content verticals (podcasts, audiobooks) and build marketplace/ad platform for sustainable margin expansion. Target: 40%+ gross margins.
- **Phase 3 (Next):** Platform flywheel — creators choose Spotify first, fans follow, data fuels better personalization, which drives more engagement and ad revenue.

## Quarterly Priorities (as of Q4 2024)

- Drive Premium ARPU growth via price increases and plan upsells (HiFi tier)
- Scale Spotify Ad Exchange for programmatic ad buying
- Expand audiobook catalog and bundled offering internationally
- Improve gross margin toward 40% target (reached 32.2% in Q4 2024, up from 26.7% YoY)
