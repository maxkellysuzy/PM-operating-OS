# Agents

Agents are generated by setup from templates and deployed to `~/.cursor/agents/`. The onboarding agent lives in `.cursor/agents/` (project-local).

---

## Onboarding (`.cursor/agents/onboarding.md`)

Interactive Q&A to configure PM-OS. Asks the questions from ONBOARDING.md, writes `config/pm-os-config.yaml`, and runs setup to deploy to `~/.cursor/`.

**How to run:** Open the PM-Operating-System repo in Cursor, then in chat say *"onboard"* or *"PM-OS setup"*.

---

## Company Researcher (`.cursor/agents/company-researcher.md`) — orchestrator

Orchestrates full company research by running two subagents and merging their outputs: **company-strategy** (latest 10-K, earnings, LinkedIn, press) and **company-product-signals** (job postings → product features/goals, Reddit, app stores). Used by onboarding (Step 1) and available for ad-hoc research.

**How to run:** In chat say *"research company [name]"*, *"company research for [name]"*, or *"LinkedIn and strategy for [company]"*. The orchestrator invokes both subagents and returns a merged summary (overview + strategy + product features and goals from job postings + source hints).

### Subagents (can be run standalone)

| Agent | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| **Company Strategy** (`company-strategy.md`) | Latest 10-K, earnings, LinkedIn, press → overview and strategy. Say *"company strategy for [name]"* or *"10-K summary for [name]"*. |
| **Company Product Signals** (`company-product-signals.md`) | Job postings (infer features/goals), Reddit, app stores → product features and goals. Say *"product signals for [name]"* or *"job postings for [company]"*. |

## Feedback Analyzer (generated by setup)

Analyzes customer feedback from your configured Slack channel, focuses on **feedback themes** (support issues, feature requests, UX friction, performance), and produces a PM-ready Markdown report. Generated from templates when you run setup.

**How to run:** Say *"analyze feedback"*, *"Slack feedback analysis"*, or *"customer feedback"*.

**Requires:** Slack MCP connected in Cursor with search access to your feedback channel.

---

## Weekly Planner (generated by setup)

Reads your Monday planning doc and recent Slack activity, then creates a prioritized daily/weekly plan. Sends the plan to your Slack DM and appends it to your daily standup doc.

**How to run:** Say *"plan my week"*, *"daily focus"*, or *"plan my day"*.

**Requires:** Slack MCP + Google Drive MCP. Configure `monday_planning_doc_id`, `daily_standup_doc_id`, and `dm_recipient_id` in config.

---

## Strategy Reviewer (generated by setup)

Scores your current execution against strategic pillars and goals. Reads knowledge layer files and recent agent outputs to surface alignment gaps, drift, and opportunities.

**How to run:** Say *"review strategy"*, *"strategy check"*, or *"strategy scorecard"*.

**Requires:** Knowledge layer populated (especially `strategy.md` and `metrics-and-targets.md`).

---

## Exec Update Generator (generated by setup)

Produces an executive-ready status update by pulling from feedback runs, weekly plans, decisions, and strategy reviews. Outputs a concise summary with what shipped, what's next, risks, and asks.

**How to run:** Say *"exec update"*, *"status update"*, or *"SLT summary"*.

**Requires:** Slack MCP + Google Drive MCP for full context. Works with partial context if only some agents have been run.

---

## Retrospective (generated by setup)

Reads across all memory (decisions, feedback, plans, strategy reviews) to surface patterns, strategy drift, execution velocity, and blind spots. Best run after you have 5+ memory entries.

**How to run:** Say *"retrospective"*, *"retro"*, or *"what patterns do you see?"*.

**Requires:** Memory entries from other agents (feedback, plans, decisions).
